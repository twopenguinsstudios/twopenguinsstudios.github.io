BOROPAGE

INTRODUCTION



Rich Boroway ‘73 , like no other brother, committed his life to serving the Delta Chapter of Theta Chi fraternity at RPI, his fellow fraternity brothers and the Theta Chi national organization.



Beginning with his initiation into the fraternity in 1970, Rich believed in and lived the traditions and ideals of the fraternity, inspired friendships and extended a helping hand to the vast brotherhood he considered his extended personal family. Rich ensured the perpetuation of the fraternity’s ideals after graduation by serving as a member of the Chapter’s Alumni Corporation Board, first as a board member, then as President, and finally as Treasurer for the last thirty years, all while shepherding and ensuring the success of the undergraduate brothers.



Rich’s real gift was his profound on-going personal connection and friendship with an astonishing number of alumni brothers scattered across the country.



For over fifty years, Rich networked, long before there was an internet, with an ever expanding circle of brothers and their families from Delta Chapter. Rich was able to maintain these connections using three simple techniques … his personal impromptu visits, sharing his ubiquitous Photo Albums and the distribution of his regularly updated (by hand) mailing list! The BOROPAGE intends to perpetuate two of Rich's proven techniques … his Photo Album and a mailing list of all brothers of Delta Chapter from 1965 to 2021.

ADDITIONS / CORRECTIONS



The Boropage is intended to be an interactive site, reflecting Rich Boroway’s life purpose of perpetuating connections between all brothers of Delta Chapter by sharing data and experiences. Feel free to make any of the following modifications using the form attached to the page. The new information received will be uploaded onto the site semi-annually.

  • 1. Correct errors in posted information
  • 2. Update mailing addresses
  • 3. Add email addresses (personal authorization is required to post email addresses)
  • 4. Add cell phone numbers
  • 5. Add photos with captions (photo scans must be 720 dpi minimum)
  • 6. Add tributes or memories of Rich or your fellow classmates
  • 7. Add Missing Composites (photo scans must be 720 dpi minimum)
  • 8. Make donations to the Boroway Memorial Fund for the maintenance of this web site
If you have any additions/corrections please Email Us

OPERATING INSTRUCTIONS



  • 1. The BOROPAGE has been customed designed to be viewed on a desk top computer or laptop device
  • 2. The BOROPAGE also can be viewed in a reformatted configuration on a tablet device (iPad) and a mobile device (iPhone).
  • 3. MENU ITEMS may be accessed by touching the desired Menu Item in the Directory at the top of the page.
  • 4. CLASS PAGES maybe accessed by touching the desired decade in the Directory at the top of the page and then touching the drop down for the desired class year.
  • 5. When multiple Tributes are on a Class Page, the TRIBUTES are timed for 30 seconds of viewing before advancing to the next Tribute. Tributes also may be advanced by touching the directional arrows on the Tribute.

  • 6. All COMPOSITES and PHOTOS are clickable
  • 7. COMPOSITES can be enlarged by finger spread on the image.
  • 8. Individual PHOTOS can be enlarged by touching on the image.
  • 9. Once enlarged, individual PHOTOS can be advanced to the next Photo by continuing to touch on the image.

See ADDITIONS / CORRECTIONS Section of this website to correct posted information; update/add/delete email or mailing addresses; add photos with captions; add tributes or memories; provide missing composites; and to make donations to maintain the BOROPAGE
