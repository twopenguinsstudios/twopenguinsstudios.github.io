"I can’t remember how we first became a stop on the “Boro” annual golf tour. There was little overlap of my class of 1970 with his of 1973, so I didn't get to know him at RPI. During his visits, Rich would fill us in on what the other brothers were up to and what was going on at the house. I was always amazed at how he knew and kept so closely in touch with so many brothers and their families. It was like they were all part of “his” big family. Rich was really one of a kind, who devoted his life to the brotherhood in so many ways. He helped to keep us all together and will be sorely missed."

DICK PODOLEC ‘70
