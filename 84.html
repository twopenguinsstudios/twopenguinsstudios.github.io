"Rich Boroway was a true pillar of Delta Chapter. Rich’s dedicated work on the Board, as the treasurer, public relations at times, and as an advisor to the actives was like a tribal wiseman who influenced Delta Chapter in a positive direction throughout the years. Rich set an example of brotherhood, and for many of us, made us realize the importance of Theta Chi in our lives. I think it is safe to say that Rich was proud of all of us in some way. I also think it is safe to say that we are all proud of you, Rich. Rest In Peace Brother! Thank you for being an impact player!"

- STAN KAPLITA ‘84
