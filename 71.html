"I have not mentioned it, but I was really sad to read that Rich Boroway died. Rich was a gem of a guy. He and Ray (Rumanowski) visited me in wine country. We went tasting at my wine club, then had cocktails and dinner at the Rutherford Grill. Rich organized the whole thing after my Dad had passed. I think he knew it would be good for me, and it was. In addition, he always made me feel included in all the pools that I took over for my Dad.

I’d also note that Rich gave me the appropriate praise / shame when I kept picking Tiger, and then, he finally won. It so happened that Glenda (Marty’s significant other) was visiting me in Las Vegas that weekend in 2019 and it’s a great memory. I even had a few exchanges with the other “kids” in the group. Rich just brought people together!"

- Marty Strauss ‘71 (Tribute written by his son Joel Strauss)
