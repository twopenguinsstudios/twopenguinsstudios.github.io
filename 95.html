BOROPAGE

"To me, Rich Boroway was impactful to Delta Chapter on par with one Dusty Rhodes! Steadfast and true, he was a standard bearer for what a great brother is, was, and should be. His loyalty to the Chapter never wavered during the best of times and the worst of times. He only had two colors ... cherry and white. May we all have such a passion to a life cause, in a selfless way, as Rich did for us all at Delta. Also, he was always good for a lost tooth during a Sagamore trip."

- RAY MORAN ‘95
